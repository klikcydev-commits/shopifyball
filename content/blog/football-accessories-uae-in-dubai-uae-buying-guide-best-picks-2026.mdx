## Care & Maintenance for UAE Climate

### Protecting Your Accessories in Heat and Humidity

**Washing Guidelines**:
- Wash grip socks and compression gear after each use
- Use mild detergents and cold water
- Avoid bleach which can damage moisture-wicking properties
- Air dry in shade to prevent UV damage

**Storage Tips**:
- Store in cool, dry places away from direct sunlight
- Keep bags in well-ventilated areas to prevent mold
- Clean shin guards regularly to prevent odor buildup
- Rotate accessories to extend lifespan

**Climate-Specific Care**:
- The UAE's high humidity can cause materials to degrade faster
- Store accessories in breathable containers
- Use moisture-absorbing packets in storage areas
- Regularly inspect for signs of wear or damage
