## Authenticity & Quality Checks

### Verifying Authentic Real Madrid Home Jersey

**Official Markers**:

## Care & Maintenance for UAE Climate

### Washing Guidelines

**Proper Care**:
- Wash inside out in cold water
- Use mild detergent, avoid bleach
- Hang dry in shade (never direct sunlight)
- Avoid tumble drying to prevent logo damage
- Iron on low heat if needed, avoid logos

### Storage Tips

- Store flat or hung, not folded tightly
- Keep away from direct sunlight to prevent fading
- Use breathable storage (not plastic bags)
- Avoid damp areas that can cause mold

### Climate-Specific Care

The UAE's intense sun and heat require:
- Regular washing after wear (sweat can degrade materials)
- Immediate cleaning if exposed to sand or dust
- Protection from UV rays during storage
- Proper ventilation to prevent odor buildup
