## Best Picks for UAE Players (Essentials)

- **Grip socks** – Help prevent slipping inside the boot and keep feet drier. Useful on astro and grass.
- **Shin guards** – Choose a size that covers the protection zone and stays secure with sleeves or straps.
- **Kit bag or duffel** – One with a separate compartment for boots and a bottle pocket is practical for training and matches.
- **Armband or wristbands** – Lightweight and quick to wash; good for sweat and sun.

## Care & Maintenance for UAE Climate

- **Wash after use** – Sweat and dust can damage fabrics and cause odour. Follow care labels.
- **Dry in shade** – Avoid long exposure to direct sun to prevent fading and wear.
- **Store in a dry place** – Reduce mould and smell; use a ventilated bag or shelf.
- **Rotate gear** – Alternate socks and base layers so items last longer.

These habits help football accessories last longer in hot, humid conditions.
